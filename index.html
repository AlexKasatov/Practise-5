Fresh Strawberry Tart

    TART
  1. 80g unsalted butter
  2. 45g powder sugar
  3. pinch of salt
  4. 30g egg
  5. 20g ground almonds
  6. 130g cake flour
  7. 25g bread flour
    ALMOND CREAM
  1. 50g unsalted butter
  2. 50g sugar
  3. 50g ground almonds
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1 tsp ~ Vanilla extract
  6. 6g cake flour
  7. 25g bread flour
    CUSTARD CREAM
  1. 2 egg yolks
  2. 40g sugar
  3. 12g cake flour
  4. 200ml milk
  5. vanilla beans
  6. 100ml heavy cream
  7. 8g sugar
    TOPPING
  1. chocolate
  2. strawberry
